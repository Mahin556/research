## 🕌 Madinah Sharif Jaate Waqt — Ye 3 Amanat Rakh Aao

---

### 1️⃣ Apni Duaen 🤲
> Apne dil ki tamam hajatein, pareshan haaliyaan, aur armaan —
> **Rawzah-e-Rasool ﷺ ke saamne rakh aao**
> Woh shafaat farmaenge In sha Allah

---

### 2️⃣ Apne Aazizon ka Salaam 💚
> Jo log nahi ja sake —
> **Unka salaam Huzoor ﷺ tak pohcha aao**
> *"As-salamu alaika ya Rasoolallah"* — unki taraf se bhi

---

### 3️⃣ Apne Gunaah 😔
> Sharmindagi le jaao, aankhon mein aansu le jaao —
> **Maafi maang aao**
> Allah ne farmaya:

> *"Agar woh apne aap par zulm karein, phir tumhare paas aayein aur Allah se maafi maangein, aur Rasool bhi unke liye maafi maange — toh woh Allah ko bohot Tawwab aur Raheem payenge."*
> **(Surah An-Nisa: 64)**

---

## 💬 Huda Khan ka Comment — Bilkul Sahi Baat

> *"Amanat Allah ke paas rakho — Nabi ﷺ khud ummat ke liye dua karte the, toh hume bhi Allah se hi dua karni chahiye"*

✅ **Yahi Aqeedah hai** — Wasila Rasool ﷺ ka, dua sirf Allah se 🌿

---

## 🌹 Jab Rawzah ke saamne khare ho...

> Aankhein band karo
> Dil hazir karo
> Aur bas itna kaho:

### *"As-salamu alaika ya Rasoolallah ﷺ"*
### *"Hamari shafaat farmana — Ya Rasoolallah ﷺ"*

---

**Madinah ki khushboo** — woh le jaate hain jo dil se jaate hain 🕊️💚
