---

> ## *حَيَّ عَلَى الْفَلَاح*
> ### *"Aao kamyabi ki taraf!"*
> **— Yeh alfaaz din mein 5 baar aazaan mein pukaare jaate hain**

---

## 💡 Sochne Ki Baat

> Duniya kamyabi dhundti hai —
> **Paison mein 💰**
> **Shohrat mein 🏆**
> **Taaqat mein 💪**

> Magar Allah ne kamyabi ka raasta bataya —
> ## **Namaz mein** 🕌

---

## 📖 Quran Ki Guarantee

> *"Kaamyab ho gaye woh momineen — jo apni namaz mein khushu rakhte hain."*
> **(Surah Al-Muminoon: 1-2)**

---

## ⚡ Namaz Kya Karti Hai?

| Duniya Kehti Hai | Namaz Deti Hai |
|-----------------|---------------|
| Zyada kaam karo | **Barakah milti hai waqt mein** |
| Stress manage karo | **Seedha Allah se sukoon** |
| Network banao | **Allah se connection** |
| Plan banao | **Tawakkul + direction** |
| Therapy lo | **Har roz 5 baar reset** |

## 🔥 Ek Gehri Baat

> Jab tum namaz chhorte ho —
> Tum sirf **ibadat nahi chorte**
> Tum **kamyabi ka darwaza band** kar lete ho

> Jab tum namaz padhte ho —
> Tum duniya ko nahi chorte —
> **Duniya tumhare peeche aati hai** 🌿
