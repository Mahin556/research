## 🌿 Iman — Ghatna Aur Badhna

---

## ⬆️ Iman Kab Badhta Hai?

> Allah ka kaam karo —
> **Namaz, Zikr, Quran, Sadaqah, Neki**
> Dil roshan hota hai 💚

---

## ⬇️ Iman Kab Ghatta Hai?

> Duniya mein kho jao —
> **Karobar, Baatein, Ghaflat**
> Dil dheera dheera door hota hai 🌫️

---

## ☠️ Iman Kab Chala Jaata Hai?

> Gunah karo —
> **Har gunah dil par ek daag**
> Daag par daag — dil andha ho jaata hai 🖤

---

## 📖 Rasool ﷺ Ne Farmaya:

> *"Jab banda gunah karta hai — uske dil par ek kaala nuqta pad jaata hai.*
> *Agar tauba kare — saaf ho jaata hai.*
> *Agar gunah jaari rahe — nuqte barhte jaate hain*
> *Yahan tak ke poora dil kaala ho jaata hai."*
> **(Tirmizi)**

---

## 🔄 Iman Ka Cycle

```
Allah ka Kaam ➜ Iman Badhta Hai ⬆️
      ↑                    ↓
   Tauba            Duniya mein Kho Jana
      ↑                    ↓
Iman Ghatta Hai ⬅️ Gunah ☠️
```
