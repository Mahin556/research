## 🌿 Hadith of Hanzala R.A — Iman Ka Ghatna Badhna

---

## 📖 Sahih Muslim 2750 — Kitab al-Tawbah

---

### 💬 Kya Hua Tha?

Hanzala Usaidi R.A — jo **Rasool ﷺ ke katibon mein se the** — ek din bohot ghabraaye hue Abu Bakr Siddiq R.A ke paas aaye aur kaha:

> ## *"Hanzala munafiq ho gaya!"* 😢

---

### 😨 Abu Bakr R.A Ne Pucha — Kyun?

Hanzala R.A ne kaha:

> *"Jab hum Rasool ﷺ ke paas hote hain —*
> *Jahannum aur Jannah ki baatein hoti hain —*
> *Aisa lagta hai jaise hum apni aankhon se dekh rahe hain*
> *Magar jab ghar jaate hain —*
> *Biwi, bachche, karobar mein lag jaate hain —*
> ***Bohot kuch bhool jaate hain!"***

---

### 💡 Abu Bakr R.A Ka Jawab

> *"Allah ki qasam — mera bhi yehi haal hai!"*

---

### 🕌 Phir Dono Rasool ﷺ Ke Paas Gaye

Hanzala R.A ne yahi baat Huzoor ﷺ ko batayi — toh Aap ﷺ ne farmaya:

---

## ✨ Rasool ﷺ Ka Jawab — Dil Ko Sukoon Dene Wala

> *"Aye Hanzala! Agar tumhara woh haal hamesha rehta*
> *Jo mere paas rehta hai —*
> *Toh farishte tumse raaste mein musaafa karte*
> *Magar Hanzala —*
> ## *"Saa'atan wa Saa'atan"*
> ### *"Kuch waqt aisa — kuch waqt aisa"*
> **(Sahih Muslim 2750)**

---

## 🔑 "Saa'atan wa Saa'atan" — Kya Matlab Hai?

| Waqt | Haal |
|------|------|
| ✅ **Ibaadat, Zikr, Quran** | Iman buland hota hai |
| 🏠 **Ghar, Karobar, Duniya** | Iman thoda kam hota hai |
| ⚠️ **Gunah** | Iman giرta hai |

> **Yeh utaar chadhaav — nifaaq nahi**
> **Yeh insaan hone ki nishani hai** 🌿

---

## 💔 Kitne Logo Ko Yeh Fikr Hoti Hai?

> *"Main pehle jitna achha tha — ab nahi raha"*
> *"Namaz mein pehle jaisa khushu nahi"*
> *"Dil sakht ho gaya hai"*

### ✅ Yeh Ehsaas Hi — Iman Ki Nishani Hai
> Munafiq ko kabhi yeh fikr nahi hoti 💚

---

## 🌸 Iman Kaise Taza Hota Hai?

Rasool ﷺ ne farmaya:

> *"Iman tumhare kapron ki tarah purana hota hai —*
> *Toh Allah se dua karo ke woh iman tumhare dilon mein taza kare."*
> **(Mustadrak al-Hakim)**

---

## 🤲 Dua — Iman Taza Karne Ki

> ## *اللَّهُمَّ جَدِّدِ الْإِيمَانَ فِي قَلْبِي*
> ### *"Ya Allah — mere dil mein iman taza farma."*
> **Ameen** 🤍
