## 🌿 10 Aadat — Jo Zindagi Mein Barakah Laati Hain

---

### 1️⃣ Fajr Waqt Par Parhna 🌅
> Yeh ek aadat —
> **Poore din ko roshan kar deti hai**
> Rasool ﷺ ne farmaya: *"Meri ummat ke liye subh mein barakah rakhi gayi hai"*

---

### 2️⃣ Bismillah — Har Kaam Se Pehle ✨
> Khaana ho, kaam ho, safar ho —
> **Bismillah se shuru karo**
> Barakah wahaan se shuru hoti hai jahan Allah ka naam liya jaaye

---

### 3️⃣ Thora Sa Sadaqah — Roz 💛
> Chahe ek rupaya hi kyun na ho —
> **Jo dete ho — kam nahi hota**
> Allah isko badhata hai, aur badhata hai 🌱

---

### 4️⃣ Roz Quran — Ek Ayat Bhi Sahi 📖
> Ghar roshan hota hai
> Dil mehfooz rehta hai
> **Ek ayat — magar roz**

---

### 5️⃣ Alhamdulillah — Har Haal Mein 🤍
> Jo hai uska shukar karo —
> **Allah aur deta hai**
> *"Agar shukar karo — main zaroor badhaunga"* (Ibrahim: 7)

---

### 6️⃣ Chhupi Hui Neki — Kisi Ko Bataye Baghair 🤫
> Jitna chhupaoge —
> **Utni zyada barakah milegi**
> Hidden deed — hidden blessing 🌿

---

### 7️⃣ Sajde Mein Dua 🕌
> Yahi woh lamha hai —
> **Jab tum Allah ke sabse qareeb hote ho**
> Jo dil mein ho — sajde mein maango

---

### 8️⃣ Allah Par Bharosa — Jab Plan Fail Ho 💚
> Tumhara plan nahi chala —
> **Allah ka plan chal raha tha**
> Yahi qabooliyat — barakah ka darwaza hai

---

### 9️⃣ Durood Shareef — Roz 🌹
> ## *اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ*
> Sukoon aata hai
> Duaen qabool hoti hain
> Zindagi mein noor aata hai

---

### 🔟 Yaad Rakho — Barakah Kya Hai 💡

> Barakah yeh nahi ke **tumhare paas zyada ho**
> Barakah yeh hai ke **Allah us mein blessing daal de**

> Thori cheez — magar barakah wali
> **Bahat cheez — magar khali**

---

## 🤲 Aaj Se Shuru Karo

> Saari 10 ek saath nahi —
> **Bas ek chunno — aaj se**
> Aur dekho Allah kya karta hai 🌿

---

**Barakah door nahi — sirf ek aadat ki door hai।** 🤍
