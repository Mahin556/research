## 💰 Hazrat Muhammad ﷺ ne diye 3 tips — Unlimited Rizq ke liye

### 1️⃣ Istighfar — Maafi maango

> *"Jo khub istighfar karta hai, Allah use har tang haal se rasta deta hai, har gham se nijaat deta hai, aur usse aisi jagah se rizq deta hai jahan se usse guman bhi nahi hota."*
> **(Abu Dawood)**

### 2️⃣ Silah Rahmi — Rishtedaron se achha sulook

> *"Jo chahta ho ke uski umr lamba ho aur rizq barhe, woh apne rishtedaron se achha sulook kare."*
> **(Bukhari & Muslim)**

### 3️⃣ Sadaqah — Raah-e-Khuda mein kharch karo

> *"Sadaqah dene se maal kam nahi hota."*
> **(Sahih Muslim)**

### ➕ Bonus — Durood Shareef

> Jitna zyada Durood parho, Allah ki rehmatein utni zyada nazil hoti hain 🌿

**🔑 Formula:**
> Istighfar + Silah Rahmi + Sadaqah + Durood
> = **Barkat, Sukoon aur Unlimited Rizq** In sha Allah ✨
