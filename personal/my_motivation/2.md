**"Jo Allah ne likha hai, woh hoke rahega. Na 'kash' se kuch badlega, na 'agar' se. Jo tha, woh tha — jo hoga, woh hoga. Bas itna kaho: 'Allah ne jo chaha, wahi hua.'"**

Isko thoda aur gehraai se:

### 🌿 Agar aur Kash — Dono bekar hain

| Tum kehte ho... | Haqeeqat yeh hai... |
|----------------|-------------------|
| *"Kash me yeh karta..."* | Jo hua, Allah ka faisla tha |
| *"Agar aisa hota..."* | Woh hota hi nahi jo Allah ne nahi likha |
| *"Meri galti se yeh hua"* | Taqdeer thi, tu sirf sabab tha |

### ✅ Sahi Soch
> **Koshish apne haath mein hai**
> **Nateeja Allah ke haath mein**

Koshish karo → Allah par chhor do → Jo ho, maan lo

### 💬 Jab dil dukhe, bas itna kaho:

# *قَدَرُ اللَّهِ وَمَا شَاءَ فَعَلَ*
*"Allah ne likha tha, aur jo woh chahta hai karta hai."*

Yeh words dil ko woh sukoon dete hain jo **pachtawa kabhi nahi de sakta.**
