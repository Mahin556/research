**"Agar poori duniya mil kar tujhe nuksan pahunchana chahay, toh nahi pahuncha sakti — jab tak Allah ne na likha ho. Aur agar Allah ne kuch likha hai, toh koi roka nahi sakta."**

Yeh fikr seedha dil mein utar jaati hai kyunki:

* **Khauf kam hota hai** — logon ka, halaat ka, dushman ka
* **Tawakkul mazboot hota hai** — Allah par bharosa poora ho jaata hai
* **Sukoon milta hai** — kyunki control sirf Uske haath mein hai

Yeh aslан ek Hadith se maakhuz hai — Ibn Abbas (RA) se riwayat hai ki Rasoolullah ﷺ ne farmaya:

> *"Jaan lo, agar saari ummat mill kar tujhe nafa pahunchana chahay, toh sirf utna hi nafa pohcha sakti hai jo Allah ne tere liye likh diya hai. Aur agar saari ummat mill kar tujhe nuksan pahunchana chahay, toh sirf utna hi nuksan pohcha sakti hai jo Allah ne tere liye likh diya hai."*
> **(Tirmizi)**
