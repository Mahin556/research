    * Flutter kicks
    * Shoulder taps

    * Plank hold

    * Reverse crunch
    * Toe touches
    * Dead bug

    * V-ups

    * Ab rollouts

**Finisher (1–2 minutes)**

    * 1 min plank challenge
    * 30 sec mountain climbers sprint
    * 30 sec flutter kick hold

**Cool Down (2–3 minutes)**

    * Cobra stretch
    * Child’s pose
    * Seated forward fold
    * Deep breathing

**Routine Structure Example**

    * Beginner: 1 round (15–20 min total)
    * Intermediate: 2 rounds
