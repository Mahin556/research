• Observe patterns, not single actions — mindset is revealed through repeated behavior.

• Listen more than you speak — people reveal their thinking through word choice and tone.

• Notice how they react under pressure — stress exposes true priorities and beliefs.

• Pay attention to what they complain about — complaints reveal values and insecurities.

• Watch how they treat people who are “below” them — it shows character and ego level.

• Notice decision-making style — emotional, logical, impulsive, or patient.

• Ask open-ended questions and let them explain their reasoning — mindset shows in explanation.

• Observe body language — crossed arms, eye contact, posture, micro-expressions.

• See what they consume daily — books, content, friends — environment shapes mindset.

• Most importantly, remove your assumptions — read patterns objectively, not emotionally.
