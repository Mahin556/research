### Been

* Used with: *has/have/had*

```
## 1. Have / Has + Been (Present Perfect)
*Abhi tak kuch hua hai ya chal raha hai*

1. I have been busy.
   Main busy raha hoon.

2. She has been sick.
   Woh bimar rahi hai.

3. She has been to Jaipur.
   Woh Jaipur ja chuki hai.

4. I have been to Delhi.
   Main Delhi ja chuka hoon.

5. She has been to Mumbai.
   Woh Mumbai ja chuki hai.

6. They have been to school.
   Woh school ja chuke hain.

7. I have been there.
   Main wahan ja chuka hoon.

8. There has been an error.
   Ek error aaya hai.

9. There has been a mistake.
   Ek galti hui hai.

---

## 2. Have / Has + Been + ing (Present Perfect Continuous)
*Kuch kaam pehle se chal raha hai aur abhi bhi chal raha hai*

1. They have been waiting.
   Woh wait kar rahe hain.

2. She has been sick since Monday.
   Woh Somwar se bimar hai.

---

## 3. Had + Been (Past Perfect)
*Kuch kaam ek aur purani baat se pehle ho chuka tha*

1. He had been working.
   Woh kaam kar raha tha.

2. I had been sleeping.
   Main so raha tha.

3. I had been there before.
   Main pehle wahan ja chuka tha.

4. He had been sick.
   Woh bimar tha.

5. We had been friends.
   Hum dost rahe the.

6. There had been a delay.
   Ek deri ho chuki thi.

---

## 4. Had + Been + ing (Past Perfect Continuous)
*Kuch kaam ek time tak lagaataar chal raha tha*

1. He had been sick for three days before he finally went to the doctor.
   Woh teen din se bimar tha jab woh aakhirkar doctor ke paas gaya.

2. When I called him, he had already been sick for a week.
   Jab maine use call kiya, woh pehle se ek hafte se bimar tha.

---

## 5. Should Have Been (Regret / Advice)
*Jo hona chahiye tha par nahi hua*

1. You should have been careful.
   Tumhe savdhaan rehna chahiye tha.

---

## 6. Been Showing Result / Effect
*Kuch hua aur uska asar abhi bhi hai*

1. She has been sick, so she won't come to the party tonight.
   Woh bimar rahi hai, isliye woh aaj raat party mein nahi aayegi.

---

## Quick Reference

| Pattern | Structure | Example | Hinglish |
|---|---|---|---|
| Present Perfect | have/has + been | I have been busy | Main busy raha hoon |
| Present Perfect Continuous | have/has + been + ing | They have been waiting | Woh wait kar rahe hain |
| Past Perfect | had + been | He had been sick | Woh bimar tha |
| Past Perfect Continuous | had + been + ing | He had been working for days | Woh dinon se kaam kar raha tha |
| Regret | should + have + been | You should have been careful | Savdhaan rehna chahiye tha |
| Result | has + been + so | She has been sick so she won't come | Bimar thi isliye nahi aayegi |
```
