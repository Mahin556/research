| Visa Type           | Purpose                             | Who Uses It                   | Validity                          |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **Tourist Visa**    | Travel, sightseeing, vacation       | General travelers             | Short-term (days to months)       |
| **Business Visa**   | Meetings, conferences, trade        | Business professionals        | Short to medium-term              |
| **Student Visa**    | Study in foreign country            | Students                      | Duration of course                |
| **Work Visa**       | Employment abroad                   | Skilled workers, employees    | Long-term (based on job contract) |
| **Transit Visa**    | Passing through a country           | Travelers with layovers       | Very short (24–72 hours)          |
| **Medical Visa**    | Treatment in another country        | Patients                      | Short-term (based on treatment)   |
| **Immigrant Visa**  | Permanent settlement                | People relocating permanently | Long-term / Permanent             |
| **Dependent Visa**  | Join family member abroad           | Spouse, children              | Depends on sponsor visa           |
| **Diplomatic Visa** | Official government/diplomatic work | Diplomats, officials          | Based on assignment               |
