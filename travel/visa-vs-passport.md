## Visa vs Passport

| Feature | **Passport** | **Visa** |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **What is it?** | Identity & travel document issued by your home country | Permission granted by a foreign country to enter |
| **Issued by** | Your own government | The country you want to visit |
| **Purpose** | Proves your citizenship & identity | Grants entry permission to a specific country |
| **Required for** | All international travel | Depends on bilateral agreements between countries |
| **Validity** | Usually 10 years | Varies (days to years) |
| **Format** | Booklet with photo | Stamp/sticker in passport or e-visa |
| **Always needed?** | Yes, for international travel | Not always — some countries are visa-free |

visa --> Timebound + Reason(travel,study,job etc)
