# The Complete Story of Prophet Nuh (Noah) ﷺ

---

## 🌍 Chapter 1: The World Before Nuh ﷺ

After the death of **Prophet Adam ﷺ**, his descendants were righteous and God-fearing people for many generations. They lived in peace, worshipping only One God — Allah.

However, after about **one thousand years**, Shaytan (Satan) began his deception. He whispered to the people:

> *"These great and pious elders who passed away — why not make statues of them to remember and honor them?"*

The people agreed, thinking it was simply a way to **preserve the memory** of great souls. At first, no one worshipped these statues. But slowly, **generation after generation**, people began to bow before them. What started as respect turned into **idol worship**.

Shirk (associating partners with Allah) and Kufr (disbelief) spread everywhere. The worship of **One Allah came to a complete stop.**

---

## 👨‍✈️ Chapter 2: Allah Sends Prophet Nuh ﷺ

Seeing humanity drowning in darkness, **Allah sent Prophet Nuh ﷺ** to guide them back to the right path.

In those times, human lifespans were extraordinarily long. **Nuh ﷺ himself lived for approximately 950 years.**

Allah says in **Surah Nuh:**
> *"Indeed We sent Nuh to his people — warn them before a painful punishment comes to them."*

Nuh ﷺ stood before his people and declared:
> *"O my people! I am a clear warner for you. Worship Allah alone, fear Him, and obey me — He will forgive your sins."*

---

## 📢 Chapter 3: 950 Years of Preaching

Nuh ﷺ dedicated his **entire life** to calling people toward Allah. He preached:

- **Day and night**
- **Privately** — visiting homes one by one
- **Publicly** — speaking in gatherings and open places
- With patience, kindness, and wisdom

Yet **950 years passed** and barely anyone believed. His own people mocked him, saying:

> *"If you are truthful, bring upon us the punishment you keep threatening us with!"*

Year after year, his nation grew **more arrogant, more sinful**, and more hostile toward him. Despite all efforts, only **a very small number of people** accepted faith.

---

## 🤲 Chapter 4: Allah's Final Verdict

After centuries of effort, **Allah revealed to Nuh ﷺ:**
> *"No one else from your nation will ever believe now — only those who have already believed. So do not grieve over what they do."*

When Nuh ﷺ received this news, he lost all hope in his people. With a heavy but surrendered heart, he made **dua (supplication) against them:**

> *"O my Lord! Do not leave a single disbeliever alive on this earth. Forgive all believing men and women."*

---

## 🚢 Chapter 5: The Command to Build the Ark

Allah accepted his prayer and **commanded Nuh ﷺ to build a massive ship (Ark).**

Nuh ﷺ had never built anything like it before. He did not even know where to begin. So **Allah sent Jibreel ﷺ (Angel Gabriel)** to guide and assist him.

### The Location and Preparation:
- Nuh ﷺ lived on a **mountain** — there were no trees nearby and no water
- He **planted many trees**, waited for them to grow, then **cut them down**
- He then **sat on the mountain and built the Ark** with his own hands

### The People Mocked Him:
When people saw him building a massive ship **on top of a mountain** — far from any sea — they laughed and said:

> *"What kind of madman builds a ship on a mountain?!"*

Nuh ﷺ bore their ridicule with complete patience and kept working.

---

## 📐 Chapter 6: The Dimensions of the Ark

After **100 years of hard work**, the Ark was finally complete. Its reported dimensions were:

| Measurement | Size |
|---|---|
| **Length** | 300 cubits (over 1 kilometer by some estimates) |
| **Width** | 50 cubits |
| **Height** | 30 cubits |
| **Depth** | 30 arm-lengths |
| **Floors** | 3 levels, each with its own door |

### Unique Design:
Unlike ordinary boats, this Ark was **enclosed from both top and bottom** — fully sealed to protect from the massive rainfall that was coming.

---

## 🐘 Chapter 7: Loading the Ark

Allah commanded Nuh ﷺ:

> *"Take aboard the believers, your household, and a **pair of every animal** on earth."*

So Nuh ﷺ loaded:
- ✅ All the believers
- ✅ His family members who had faith
- ✅ A male and female pair of every creature on earth

---

## 🌊 Chapter 8: The Great Flood Begins

Then **Allah unleashed water from both the sky and the earth** simultaneously. Rain poured down in torrents from above, and water gushed up from beneath the ground.

The flood was so massive that:
- It swallowed **every human and animal** on the surface of the earth
- The water level **rose above the highest mountains**
- **Nothing survived** except those inside the Ark

The Ark sailed powerfully through enormous waves, kept safe by Allah's command.

---

## 💔 Chapter 9: The Heartbreaking Moment — Nuh's Son

Despite being the son of one of the greatest Prophets, **Nuh ﷺ's own son refused to board the Ark.**

Nuh ﷺ called out to him desperately:
> *"O my son! Come aboard with us and do not be among the disbelievers!"*

His son replied arrogantly:
> *"I will take refuge on top of a mountain — it will protect me from this water."*

Nuh ﷺ warned him:
> *"Today, nothing will save anyone from Allah's command — except those upon whom He shows mercy."*

Before they could say another word, **a massive wave came and drowned his son** right before his eyes.

---

## 😢 Chapter 10: Nuh ﷺ Questions Allah

Watching his own son drown shattered Nuh ﷺ's heart. With tears in his eyes, he called out to Allah:

> *"O Allah! You promised me that my household would be saved. And You are the most just of all judges — it is impossible that Your promise goes unfulfilled."*

Allah responded with a profound truth:
> *"O Nuh! He was NOT from your household — because he was a disbeliever. Disbelief severs all ties of relation."*

Then Allah warned him:
> *"Do not ask Me for things about which you have no knowledge."*

Nuh ﷺ immediately understood his mistake. He repented at once and said:

> *"O my Lord! I seek Your refuge from asking You what I do not know. If You do not forgive me and have mercy on me, I will be among the losers."*

This shows the **humility and immediate repentance** of a true Prophet.

---

## 🌤️ Chapter 11: The Storm Ends

The people on the Ark spent **6 months** riding the floodwaters. Then, by Allah's command:

- The storm **stopped**
- Allah commanded the earth to **swallow all the water**
- The land dried up completely
- The Ark came to **rest on a mountain**

Allah then called out:

> *"Descend with peace and blessings — upon you and upon the generations that will come from you."*

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Chapter 12: A New Beginning — The Origin of Humanity

After the flood, **only Nuh ﷺ and the believers remained** on earth. All of today's humanity descends from this small group. Even all animal species were repopulated from the pairs saved on the Ark.

### Nuh ﷺ Had Three Sons:

| Son | Descendants | Region |
|---|---|---|
| **Saam (Shem)** | Wheat/olive complexion | Arabs, Syrians, Palestinians, Israelis |
| **Haam (Ham)** | Dark complexion | Africans, Aboriginal Australians, Indians, Egyptians, Sudanese |
| **Yaafith (Japheth)** | Fair complexion | Chinese, Japanese, Koreans, Europeans |

His **fourth son, Kina'an**, who drowned due to disbelief, left **no descendants** behind.

---

## 📖 Chapter 13: What the Quran Says

Allah says in the Holy Quran:

> *"Nuh called upon Us — and how excellent are those who respond to prayers! We saved him and his family from the great distress, and We made his descendants the surviving ones."*

---

## 🏔️ Chapter 14: The Ark Found in Turkey

After the flood, Nuh ﷺ **lived for another 350 years** before passing away.

Most remarkably — **researchers have discovered the remains of the Ark in Turkey**, on Mount Judi/Ararat. This is a **physical, archaeological evidence** that confirms this incredible story.

Allah says in the Quran:
> *"And indeed We have left this as a Sign — so is there anyone who will take heed?"*

> Stories can be denied. **Evidence cannot.**
