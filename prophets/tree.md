# The 25 Prophets Mentioned in the Quran — Complete Guide

---

## 1️⃣ Adam ﷺ — The First Human & First Prophet

**Sent to:** All of humanity (Earth)

### Creation:
- Created by Allah from **different types of soil**, mixed with water by angels and shaped into a human form
- After **40 years**, the clay hardened. On the **10th of Muharram, a Friday**, Allah gave him a soul
- Allah taught him the **knowledge of everything**
- All angels **prostrated to Adam** — except **Iblees (Satan)** who refused out of arrogance and was cast out of Paradise

### Life on Earth:
- Married **Hawwa (Eve)**, who was created in Paradise
- Both were sent to Earth after eating from the **forbidden tree**
- Adam repented sincerely — Allah **accepted his repentance** (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:37)
- Met Hawwa again at **Arafat** and together they built the **Ka'bah**
- Given a **book of 10 pages**, taught people faith, worship and knowledge

### Tragedy:
- His son **Qabeel (Cain)** killed **Habeel (Abel)** out of jealousy — **the first murder in human history**

### Key Facts:
| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| Lifespan | ~960 years |
| Height | 30–40 metres (narrations) |
| Grave | Jerusalem, Mina, or Arafat (disputed) |

---

## 2️⃣ Idris ﷺ — The Prophet of Knowledge & Art

**Sent to:** Babylon → Egypt

### Special Qualities:
- **5th generation** descendant of Adam ﷺ
- Given **30 Suhuf** (small scriptures) by Allah
- Knew **72 languages** and invited every community in their own tongue

### Contributions to Humanity:
- **First person to write** and use a pen
- Taught people how to **sew clothes** (before him people wore animal skins)
- Taught people how to **build cities**
- Studied **stars and astronomy**

### Unique Status:
- The Quran says: *"We raised him to a high place"*
- Many scholars believe he was **taken to the heavens without dying**
- Said to be in the **4th level of the sky**

---

## 3️⃣ Nuh ﷺ — The Second Adam

**Sent to:** Mosul region, Iraq (Mesopotamia)

### Mission:
- People had fallen into **idol worship, injustice and immorality**
- Received prophethood at **age 50**
- Preached for **950 years** — only a small number believed

### The Great Flood:
- Allah commanded him to build a massive Ark
- **Angel Jibreel** came to help him
- Ark dimensions: **300 cubits long, 50 wide, 30 high** (over 1km by some estimates), **3 floors**
- Loaded believers + **a pair of every animal**
- Flood lasted **6 months** — water rose above mountains
- Ark rested on **Mount Judi**

### His Sons:

| Son | Descendants |
|---|---|
| **Saam** | Arabs, Jews, Persians, Syrians, Babylonians |
| **Haam** | Africans, Egyptians, Ethiopians |
| **Yaafith** | Turks, Greeks, Russians, Chinese, Indians, Europeans |

- His **4th son Kina'an** drowned because he refused to board
- Lived to age **1,000** — known as the **"Second Adam"**

---

## 4️⃣ Hud ﷺ — Prophet to the People of Aad

**Sent to:** People of **Aad — Yemen**

### The People of Aad:
- Wealthy traders who built **tall buildings**
- Became **corrupt, arrogant**, cheated in trade
- Worshipped idols

### What Happened:
- Hud ﷺ called them to Allah — they **denied him**
- Allah first sent **drought and famine** as a warning
- Then sent a **fierce burning wind** that destroyed them completely
- Only Hud ﷺ and the believers were saved
- Hud ﷺ later **settled and passed away in Mecca**

---

## 5️⃣ Salih ﷺ — Prophet to the People of Thamud

**Sent to:** People of **Thamud — Madain Salih, Saudi Arabia**

### The People of Thamud:
- Advanced in **technology and architecture** — carved homes inside mountains
- Idol worshippers

### The She-Camel Miracle:
- People challenged: *"Bring a pregnant she-camel from this rock and we'll believe"*
- Salih ﷺ prayed — **a camel emerged from the rock** miraculously
- Its milk was so abundant **the entire tribe could drink from it**
- Leaders feared losing influence and **secretly killed the camel at night**

### Punishment:
- Given **3-day warning**
- Salih ﷺ and believers left the city
- On the **3rd day** — a **massive earthquake and thunderous sound** destroyed them all

## 6️⃣ Ibrahim ﷺ — The Close Friend of Allah (Khalilullah)

**Sent to:** The Chaldeans (Kaldani) — Iraq/Babylon

### Standing Against Idol Worship:
- While people were at a festival, Ibrahim ﷺ **smashed all idols except the largest**
- Hung the axe around the big idol's neck
- When questioned, said: *"Ask the big one — if he can speak"*
- Defeated them with **pure logic and wisdom**
- They threw him into a **massive fire** — but Allah made it **cool and safe for him**

### His Family & Legacy:
- Migrated to **Egypt**, then **Palestine**
- Had son **Ismail** from Hajar → ancestor of **Arabs and Prophet Muhammad ﷺ**
- Had son **Ishaq** from Sarah → ancestor of **Jews and Bani Israel prophets**
- Built the **Ka'bah** with Ismail ﷺ
- Established **Hajj**
- Passed the greatest test — willingness to **sacrifice his son**
- Allah replaced his son with an animal → origin of **Eid ul-Adha**

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| Age at death | 175 years |
| Buried | Hebron (Al-Khalil), Palestine |
| Title | Khalilullah — Close Friend of Allah |

## 7️⃣ Lut ﷺ — Prophet to the People of Sodom

**Sent to:** People of **Sodom — present-day Jordan / Dead Sea region**

### The Crimes of Sodom:
- Not merely private sins — these were **organized crimes**:
  - Forced homosexual acts on others
  - **Kidnapped male travelers** to abuse them
  - **Enslaved young boys** and treated them as property
  - Blocked roads and committed assaults publicly

### The Angels' Visit:
- **Three angels** came in the form of handsome young men
- The crowd gathered to assault them
- Angels revealed their identity to Lut ﷺ and told him to **flee at night**

### Punishment:
- The city was **turned upside down**
- **Stones rained from the sky**
- The entire corrupt nation was destroyed

> **Note:** The Quran clarifies they were punished for **organized oppression and forcing sin on society**, not merely private acts.

## 8️⃣ Ismail ﷺ — The Patient, Faithful Son

**Sent to:** Tribe of **Jurham — Mecca**

- Eldest son of Ibrahim ﷺ, mother was Hajar
- Left as an infant in the **Valley of Mecca** — the miracle of **Zamzam water** emerged when Hajar ran between Safa and Marwa
- Known for **archery and horse riding**
- Said to have **revived and established the Arabic language**
- **Miracles:** Made fruits grow on thorny trees, caused sheep to give milk
- Helped Ibrahim ﷺ build the **Ka'bah**
- Passed away in Mecca — **buried next to the Ka'bah** in the area of Hijr Ismail

---

## 9️⃣ Ishaq ﷺ — The Righteous Son of Ibrahim

**Sent to:** People of **Canaan — Palestine**

- Second son of Ibrahim ﷺ, mother was Sarah
- Father of **Ya'qub (Jacob)** ﷺ
- Described in the Quran as: *"good, righteous, truthful, devoted to Allah"*
- **Miracles:** Animals testifying to his prophethood, moving a mountain through prayer, reviving dead animals
- Passed away at **age 120** in Palestine
- Buried in **Hebron** alongside his father Ibrahim ﷺ

---

## 🔟 Ya'qub ﷺ — Israel, Father of 12 Tribes

**Sent to:** People of **Canaan — Palestine**

- Grandson of Ibrahim ﷺ, son of Ishaq ﷺ
- Given the title **"Israel"** — his descendants are called **Bani Israel**
- Had **12 sons** → the 12 tribes of the Israelites
- Endured the great test of **losing his son Yusuf**
- Never complained — said: *"I only express my sorrow to Allah"* (Surah Yusuf 86)
- On his deathbed asked his sons who they would worship → they replied: *"The One God — God of Ibrahim, Ismail and Ishaq"*
- Died in **Egypt** but was taken to **Palestine** per his will, buried next to Ishaq ﷺ

## 1️⃣1️⃣ Yusuf ﷺ — The Most Beautiful Story in the Quran

**Sent to:** People of **Egypt**

### His Journey:
| Stage | Event |
|---|---|
| Childhood | Saw dream of 11 stars + sun + moon prostrating to him |
| Betrayal | Brothers threw him in a well out of jealousy |
| Slavery | Sold to Aziz, chief minister of Egypt |
| Trial | Minister's wife Zulaikha tried to tempt him — he refused |
| Prison | Imprisoned unfairly — preached Tawheed to fellow prisoners |
| Rise | Interpreted Pharaoh's dream: 7 years abundance + 7 years famine |
| Power | Made treasurer and ruler of Egypt's resources |
| Reunion | Forgave his brothers: *"No blame on you today. May Allah forgive you"* (Surah Yusuf 92) |

- His final prayer: *"Let me die as a Muslim and join me with the righteous"*
- Buried near **Jerusalem**

## 1️⃣2️⃣ Shu'ayb ﷺ — The Orator of the Prophets

**Sent to:** People of **Madyan** (Jordan/NW Saudi Arabia) and **Aykah**

- Known for **sweet and effective speech** — called *"Orator of the Prophets"*
- His people were traders who: cheated in weights, charged interest, committed fraud
- He preached: *"Give full measure and weight with justice"* (Surah Al-Araf 85)
- **Madyan's punishment:** Violent earthquake — found dead in their homes
- **Aykah's punishment:** Days of extreme heat, then a burning fire rained from sky
- Met **Musa ﷺ** who married one of his daughters
- Lived **242 years** — shrine in **Wadi Shu'ayb, Jordan**

## 1️⃣3️⃣ Ayyub ﷺ — The Prophet of Patience

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Şanlıurfa region, Turkey**

### The Greatest Test:
Lost **everything** in sequence:
1. His **wealth**
2. His **children**
3. His **health** (body covered in painful sores)

Only his **wife** stayed and cared for him. Despite Shaytan's whispers, he **never complained to anyone but Allah**.

### The Miracle of Healing:
- After **7 years** of illness, Allah told him: *"Strike your foot on the ground"*
- A **spring of water appeared** — he drank and washed → completely cured
- Allah **restored his wealth and children**

> **Lesson:** Never lose hope. True patience means trusting Allah completely in every hardship.

## 1️⃣4️⃣ Dhul-Kifl ﷺ — The One With Responsibility

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Greater Syria (Sham)**

- Cousin of Alyasa ﷺ
- Continued the mission of Musa ﷺ after his death
- Known for: patience in worship, calmness in judgment, absolute fairness
- Title **"Dhul-Kifl"** means *"The one who took on responsibility"*
- Never failed in his duty despite Shaytan's tricks
- Mentioned in **Surah Al-Anbiya and Surah Sad**

## 1️⃣5️⃣ Musa ﷺ — The One Who Spoke to Allah (Kalimullah)

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Egypt**

### Birth & Early Life:
- Born when Pharaoh was killing all Bani Israel newborn boys
- Allah inspired his mother: *"Place him in the river — We will return him to you"* (Surah Al-Qasas 28:7)
- Found by **Pharaoh's wife Asiya** — raised in the palace
- His real mother brought as his **nurse** (Allah's promise fulfilled)

### Prophethood & Miracles:
- Received prophethood on **Mount Tur (Sinai)**
- **Staff turning into a snake**
- **Hand shining bright white**
- **Defeated all of Pharaoh's magicians** — they immediately believed and accepted martyrdom

### The Exodus:
- Led Bani Israel out of Egypt
- Pharaoh chased with a massive army
- Musa ﷺ struck the sea → **sea split in two** → Israelites crossed safely
- Pharaoh's army drowned
- Allah said: *"Today We preserve your body as a sign"* (Surah Yunus 92)
- Received the **Tawrat (Torah)** on Mount Tur
- During his absence, Samiri led people to worship a **golden calf**
- Spent **40 years in the desert** — forbidden from entering the holy land
- Passed away in the **desert** — grave said to be near **Jerusalem**

## 1️⃣6️⃣ Harun ﷺ — The Eloquent Brother

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Egypt**

- Brother of Musa ﷺ — chosen as prophet alongside him
- Had a **softer heart** and was **more eloquent in speech** than Musa ﷺ
- Went with Musa ﷺ to Pharaoh
- Led the people while Musa ﷺ was on Mount Tur
- Could not stop the people from worshipping the golden calf
- Passed away **before Musa ﷺ** — buried on **Jabal Harun (Mountain of Harun)**

## 1️⃣7️⃣ Dawud ﷺ — The Prophet King

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Jerusalem**

- Given the **Zabur (Psalms)** — had a **powerful, beautiful voice**
- Killed **Jalut (Goliath)** as a soldier in Talut's army → became **King**
- Ruled with **justice**, made Jerusalem the capital
- **Miracle:** Could shape **iron without fire**
- Fasted every other day and worshipped all night
- Father of **Sulaiman ﷺ**
- Born, lived and buried in **Jerusalem**

## 1️⃣8️⃣ Sulaiman ﷺ — The Greatest Kingdom

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Jerusalem**

### Powers Given by Allah:
- **Commanded the wind**
- **Communicated with animals and jinn**
- **Understood all animal languages**
- Jinn worked for him — building palaces, castles, diving for pearls

### Famous Story — Queen Bilqis of Saba:
- The **Hoopoe bird** informed him that the people of Saba were worshipping the sun
- Sulaiman ﷺ sent a letter inviting Queen Bilqis to Islam
- Had her **throne transported instantly** before she arrived
- She saw the wonders and declared: *"I submit with Sulaiman to Allah, Lord of the worlds"* (Surah An-Naml 44)

### Death:
- Died leaning on his staff — **no one knew** until the staff broke and he fell
- This proved to the jinn that **they do not know the unseen**
- Buried near **Bait Al-Maqdis, Jerusalem**

## 1️⃣9️⃣ Ilyas ﷺ — Prophet to Lebanon

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **present-day Lebanon**

- Possibly from the family line of **Harun ﷺ**
- People were worshipping a false god called **Ba'al**
- When they repented, he prayed → **rain came and blessings returned**
- They went astray again — this time their denial was stronger
- Miraculously **healed a sick boy** in a village — that boy (Alyasa) later became a prophet
- Shrine in **Wadi Shu'ayb, Jordan** (according to narrations)

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Sham/Aleppo region**

- Known as **Elisha** in the Torah
- Continued the mission of **Ilyas ﷺ**
- **Miracles:** Healed the sick, made food and water abundant during famine

## 2️⃣1️⃣ Yunus ﷺ — The Man of the Whale

**Sent to:** City of **Ninawa (Nineveh)** — Assyrian Empire (modern Iraq)

### The Story:
- Preached Tawheed for years — people rejected him
- Left the city **without Allah's permission** — a mistake in judgment
- Boarded a ship during a **violent storm** — lots were drawn, his name came up 3 times
- Was **thrown into the sea** — swallowed by a **giant whale**

### The Prayer Inside the Whale:
> *"La ilaha illa anta, subhanaka, inni kuntu minaz-zalimin"*
> *"There is no god but You, Glory be to You, indeed I was among the wrongdoers"* (Surah Al-Anbiya 87)

- His repentance was accepted — fish **brought him safely to shore**

### The Miracle of Ninawa:
- While Yunus ﷺ was away, the **entire city of Ninawa repented collectively**
- They are the **only nation in the Quran saved as a whole** through mass repentance (Surah Yunus 98)
- Yunus ﷺ returned and they welcomed him with joy
- Passed away at **age 83**

> **Lesson:** A nation that repents together can be saved together. Never despair of Allah's mercy.

## 2️⃣2️⃣ Zakariyya ﷺ — The Carpenter Prophet

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Jerusalem (Bait Al-Maqdis)**

- Worked as a **carpenter** and served in Bait Al-Maqdis
- Raised and took care of **Maryam** with great devotion
- Saw miraculous food appearing for Maryam in her prayer place → made a heartfelt dua
- Even in **old age**, Allah blessed him with a son — **Yahya ﷺ**
- Was **martyred** — the Jews cut down the tree where he had hidden
- Shrine believed to be in **Aleppo, Syria**

## 2️⃣3️⃣ Yahya ﷺ — The Pure and Righteous

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Palestine, Jerusalem, Damascus**

- Son of Zakariyya ﷺ — born as an **answer to his father's prayer in old age**
- Learned the **Tawrat at a young age**
- **Pure, righteous, deeply devoted to Allah**
- Supported and called people to follow **Isa ﷺ**
- Imprisoned due to a queen's request
- **Martyred at age 34** because he refused to approve King Herod's unlawful marriage
- **Miracles:** A rock spoke, stars were visible in daylight
- Grave inside the **Umayyad Mosque, Damascus**

**Sent to:** Bani Israel — **Palestine**

### Miraculous Birth:
- Born to **Maryam** without a father — by Allah's direct command
- *"Allah breathed into Maryam from His spirit"* (Surah Al-Anbiya 91)
- Born under a **palm tree** in a quiet place

### Miracles:
- **Spoke from the cradle** defending his mother
- **Healed the sick**
- **Brought the dead back to life** by Allah's permission
- Given the **Injil (Gospel)**

### Islamic vs Common Belief:

## 2️⃣5️⃣ Muhammad ﷺ — The Final Messenger, Mercy to All Worlds

**Sent to:** All of humanity — born in **Makkah, 570 CE**

### Early Life:
- Father **Abdullah** passed before his birth
- Mother **Aminah** passed when he was **6 years old**
- Raised by grandfather, then uncle **Abu Talib**
- Known in Mecca as **"Al-Amin"** — The Trustworthy
- **Never worshipped idols, never committed any sin**
- Married **Khadijah RA** at age 25

### Prophethood:
- At **age 40**, in the **Cave of Hira** — received first revelation: *"Read"* (Surah Al-Alaq)
- First believers: **Khadijah, Abu Bakr, Ali, Zaid ibn Haritha RA**

### Key Life Events:

| Event | Year |
|---|---|
| First Revelation | 610 CE |
| Year of Sorrow (Khadijah & Abu Talib died) | 619 CE |
| Isra wal Mi'raj (Night Journey to heavens) | 621 CE |
| Migration to Madinah (Hijra) | 622 CE |
| Battle of Badr | 624 CE |
| Battle of Uhud | 625 CE |
| Conquest of Makkah | 630 CE |
| Farewell Sermon (140,000+ companions) | 632 CE |
| Passed away | 632 CE |

### His Legacy:
- Ended **tribal revenge and racism**
- Established **women's rights**
- Left the **Quran and Sunnah** as eternal guidance
- His greatest miracle: **The Quran** — an unlettered man brought a book no one has ever matched
- Allah's challenge stands: *"Bring even one chapter like it"* — **never achieved in 1,400+ years**
- Buried in his room — now inside **Masjid An-Nabawi, Madinah**
