## What is Maghrib?
- **Maghrib** is the **fourth daily prayer** in Islam. 
- It is prayed **immediately after sunset**.
- It marks the end of the daytime.
---
## Time of Maghrib
- **Starts:** Immediately after the sun completely sets. 
- **Ends:** When the red twilight (afterglow) disappears.
⚠️ It is recommended to pray Maghrib **early**, not delay it.
---
## Rak‘ahs in Maghrib

| Type                | Rak‘ahs | Status        |
| ------------------- | ------- | ------------- |
| Fard                | 3       | Obligatory    |
| Sunnah (Mu’akkadah) | 2       | Strong Sunnah |
| Nafl (Optional)     | 2       | Optional      |

### Total = 7 Rak‘ahs (Minimum required = 3 Fard)
---
## Importance of Maghrib
- Should be prayed promptly. 
- The Prophet ﷺ used to pray Maghrib soon after sunset.
- It is one of the prayers recited **aloud**.

---
## After Maghrib
- Nafl allowed.
- But once Isha time enters → Maghrib time ends.
