• **Eid Namaz** is a special prayer performed by Muslims on the two Islamic festivals:
- **Eid al-Fitr** – celebrated after Ramadan 
- **Eid al-Adha** – celebrated during Dhul Hijjah
• It is prayed in congregation, usually in a **mosque** or open ground (Eidgah).
---
### Time of Eid Namaz
• After sunrise (about 15–20 minutes after)  
• Before Zuhr prayer  
• Cannot be prayed before sunrise

---
### How Many Rakats?
• **2 Rakats (units of prayer)**  
• With extra **Takbeers**

---
### Method of Eid Namaz (Hanafi method – simple steps)
• Make intention (Niyyah) for Eid prayer
#### First Rakat:
• Say **Allahu Akbar** (start prayer)  
• Fold hands and read **Sana**  
• Say **3 extra Takbeers**
- After each Takbeer, raise hands
- Leave hands at sides after 1st and 2nd
- After 3rd, fold hands again  
    • Imam recites Surah Al-Fatiha and another Surah  
    • Complete rakat normally
#### Second Rakat:
• After Surah recitation  
• Say **3 extra Takbeers** (raise hands each time)  
• On 4th Takbeer go into Ruku  
• Complete prayer normally

---
### Khutbah (Sermon)
• After the prayer, Imam gives **Khutbah**  
• Listening is wajib (important)

---
### Sunnah Before Eid Prayer
• Take bath (Ghusl)  
• Wear clean or new clothes  
• Apply perfume (for men)  
• Eat something sweet before **Eid al-Fitr** prayer  
• Do NOT eat before **Eid al-Adha** prayer  
• Recite Takbeer while going to prayer
