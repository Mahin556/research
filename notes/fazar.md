## What is Fajr?

- **Fajr** is the **first prayer** of the five daily prayers in Islam.
- It is offered **before sunrise**, at dawn.
- It marks the beginning of the Islamic day.

---

## Time of Fajr
- Starts: **True dawn (Subh Sadiq)** – when light spreads horizontally across the sky. 
- Ends: **Just before sunrise**.
- If the sun rises → Fajr time is over.

---

## Rak‘ahs in Fajr

| Type                | Rak‘ahs | Status            |
| ------------------- | ------- | ----------------- |
| Sunnah (Mu’akkadah) | 2       | Highly emphasized |
| Fard                | 2       | Obligatory        |
### Total = **4 Rak‘ahs**

---

## Importance of Fajr

- Very beloved prayer.
- The Prophet ﷺ said the **2 Sunnah of Fajr are better than the world and everything in it**.
- Angels witness Fajr prayer.
- Praying Fajr in congregation has great reward.

---
## Special Notes

- No Nafl prayer after Fajr until sunrise.   
- If missed:
    - Pray Sunnah + Fard after sunrise.
- Best prayed in congregation.
