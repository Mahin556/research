## 🌟 Pehli Baat — Seedha Sawaal

Ibrahim (AS) ka tareeqa tha ke woh pehle **seedha sawaal** karte the — argue nahi karte the, seedha sochne par majboor karte the:

> **"Kya yeh patthar tumhe sun sakte hain?"**

- Qaum hans padi — unhe lagaa yeh mazhak hai
- Lekin Ibrahim (AS) ne hansne nahi diya — **jawab maanga**

---

## 📢 Ibrahim (AS) Ki Mukammal Dawat — Point by Point

### 1️⃣ Butoon Ki Bekaari Par Sawaal

Ibrahim (AS) apni qaum se kehte:

> **"Yeh but na tumhara nuksaan rok sakte hain, na tumhe koi faida de sakte hain — phir bhi tum unki ibadat karte ho?"**

- Yeh ek **logical argument** tha
- Agar koi cheez na faida de sake na nuksaan — toh woh **ibadat ke laayaq kaise ho sakti hai?**
- Qaum ke paas koi jawab nahi tha
- Woh sirf kehte: **"Yeh hamare baap dada ke maane hue mabood hain"**
- Ibrahim (AS) ne jawaab diya: **"Kya baap dada galat the toh bhi unki pairwi karoge?"**

---

### 2️⃣ Chand, Sitaaron Aur Suraj Ki Haqeeqat Batai

Jab Ibrahim (AS) cave se nikle — unhone khud yeh safar tay kiya:

**Sitara dekha:**
> *"Yeh mera Rabb hai?"* — phir doob gaya — kaha: **"Doob jaane wala mera Rabb nahi ho sakta"**

**Chaand dekha:**
> *"Yeh mera Rabb hai?"* — phir gaayab hua — kaha: **"Jo ghaaib ho jaaye woh mera Rabb nahi"**

**Suraj dekha:**
> *"Yeh sabse bada hai — yeh mera Rabb hai?"* — phir doob gaya — kaha:
> **"Main un sab se baree hoon jo tum Allah ke saath shareek banate ho"**

### Is Argument Ka Maqsad:
- Apni qaum ko dikhana ke **jo cheez khud fani hai woh Khuda nahi ho sakti**
- Khuda woh hai jo **hamesha hai, kabhi nahi doobta, kabhi nahi chhupta**

---

### 3️⃣ Apne Walid Azar Ko Dawat Di — Sab Se Mushkil Baat

Ibrahim (AS) ka walid **Azar** but banane wali factory ka maalik tha — yani but parasti ka sabse bada sardar.

Ibrahim (AS) ne apne baap se kaha:

> **"Abba jaan, aap un cheezoon ki ibadat kyun karte ho jo na sun sakti hain, na dekh sakti hain, aur na aapke kisi kaam aa sakti hain?"**

Phir kaha:

> **"Abba jaan, mujhe woh ilm mila hai jo aapko nahi mila — mere saath chaliye, main aapko seedha raasta dikhaunga"**

Phir kaha:

> **"Abba jaan, Shaytan ki ibadat mat kariye — Shaytan Rehman ka naafarmaan hai"**

Phir kaha:

> **"Abba jaan, mujhe darr hai ke aap par Allah ka azaab na aaye aur aap Shaytan ke dost ban jaayein"**

### Azar Ka Jawab:
- Woh gusse mein aagaya
- Bola: **"Aye Ibrahim kya tu mere khudaon se nahi darta? Agar tu baaz nahi aaya toh main tujhe patthar maar maar ke bhaga dunga"**
- Ibrahim (AS) ne jawab diya: **"Aap par salaam ho — main apne Rabb se aapke liye maghfirat maangunga"**

> **Yeh Ibrahim (AS) ki mohabbat, sabr, aur adab ka sabse bada saboot tha — baap ne dhamki di aur bete ne dua di.**

---

### 4️⃣ Poori Qaum Ko Tawheed Ki Dawat Di

Ibrahim (AS) ghar ghar jaate, har darwaze tak pahunchte aur kehte:

> **"Tumhara Rabb woh hai jisne aasman, zameen, sitaare, suraj — sab kuch paida kiya"**

- Din bhar aur raat gehra tak yeh silsila chalta
- Log sunne se inkaar karte
- Kuch hanste
- Kuch gusse mein aate
- Lekin **Ibrahim (AS) rukke nahi**

---

### 5️⃣ Butoon Ko Todne Ke Baad Qaum Ko Jawab Diya

Jab qaum ne pucha: **"Ibrahim kya tune yeh buth thode hain?"**

Ibrahim (AS) ne kaha:

> **"Yeh sabse bada but toh sahi salamat khada hai — isse pooch lo, shayad yahi bataye kisne thoda"**

Qaum boli: **"Tum jaante ho yeh bol nahi sakta!"**

Ibrahim (AS) ne woh jawab diya jisne **poore shehar ki deewaron ko hila diya:**

> **"Jab tum khud jaante ho ke yeh bol nahi sakta, chal nahi sakta, faida nahi de sakta, nuksaan nahi pahuncha sakta — toh phir tum inhe Rabb kyun banaate ho?!"**

- **Yeh ek aisa jawab tha jiska koi radd nahi tha**
- Log andar se jaante the ke Ibrahim sach keh rahe hain
- Lekin takabbur aur baap dada ki riwayat ne unhe rokhe rakha

---

### 6️⃣ Namrood Ke Darbaar Mein Sabse Badi Daleel

Jab Namrood ne kaha: **"Main khuda hoon — main zindagi aur maut deta hoon"**

Ibrahim (AS) ne kaha:

> **"Mera Rabb woh hai jo zindagi deta hai aur maut deta hai"**

Namrood ne do qaidion ko bulaya — ek ko maara, ek ko choda — kaha: **"Dekho main bhi zindagi aur maut deta hoon"**

Ibrahim (AS) ne woh sawaal kiya jisne **duniya ke sabse bade badshah ko poore darbaar ke saamne bebaas kar diya:**

> **"Beshak mera Allah suraj ko Mashriq se nikalta hai — agar tu khuda hai toh isse Maghrib se nikal kar dikha!"**

- Darbaar mein **khamoshi chha gayi**
- Ek patthar tak girne ki aawaaz sunai deti
- Namrood ke paas **koi jawab nahi tha**
- Quran kehta hai: **"Kafir behaal hokar reh gaya"**

## 🔥 Ibrahim (AS) Ka Tareeqa-e-Dawat — Kya Khaas Tha?

| Khasiyat | Misal |
|---|---|
| **Sawaal se shuru** | "Kya yeh patthar sun sakte hain?" |
| **Logic use kiya** | "Jo doob jaaye woh Rabb nahi" |
| **Adab ke saath** | Baap ko bhi "Abba jaan" keh ke samjhaya |
| **Himmat ke saath** | Namrood ke darbaar mein akele khade rahe |
| **Baar baar koshish** | Din raat har darwaze tak gaye |
| **Practical proof** | But thod ke dikhaya ke woh kuch nahi kar sakte |
| **Ek hi sawaal** | "Suraj ko Maghrib se nikalo" — jawab impossible tha |

## 💔 Qaum Ka Jawab — Har Baar Inkaar

Qaum ne alag alag bahane diye:

| Ibrahim (AS) Ki Baat | Qaum Ka Jawab |
|---|---|
| Yeh but kuch nahi kar sakte | Yeh hamare baap dada ke mabood hain |
| Inki ibadat kyun? | Yeh hamari riwayat hai |
| But todne ke baad — inse pooch lo | Tum jaante ho yeh bol nahi sakta |
| Phir inki ibadat kyun? | **Koi jawab nahi — gusse mein aa gaye** |
| Suraj Maghrib se nikalo | **Bilkul khamosh — bebaas** |

## 🤲 Ibrahim (AS) Ki Dua Apni Qaum Ke Liye

Ibrahim (AS) sirf bolte nahi the — **dua bhi karte the:**

> **"Aye Rabb mujhe aur mere walidain ko aur sabhi mominon ko Qayamat ke din maaf farma"**

- Woh apne inkaar karne wale baap ke liye bhi **dua maangte rahe**
- Yeh dikhata hai ke dawat sirf zuban se nahi — **dil se bhi hoti hai**

---

## 📌 Sabse Bada Sabaq Ibrahim (AS) Ki Dawat Se

> **Ibrahim (AS) ne kabhi ek baar mein haar nahi maani.**

- Baap ne dhamki di — **phir bhi dua di**
- Qaum ne mazaak udaya — **phir bhi samjhaya**
- Namrood ne aag mein dala — **phir bhi maidan nahi chhoda**
- Har jagah se nikaala gaya — **phir bhi dawat jaari rakhi**

> **Yeh hai asli dawat — jo result ki parwah kiye bagair, sirf Allah ki khushi ke liye di jaaye.**
