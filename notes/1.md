# Why Muslim Celebrate Eid ul-Adha?

---

## 🌟 Background

- This story is the **true origin of Eid ul-Adha (Bakri Eid)**
- Ibrahim (AS) received his son Ismail (AS) after **86 years** of a childless life, after years of prayers and patience
- This made Ismail (AS) the most precious thing in his life

---

## 🌙 The Divine Dream

- Ibrahim (AS) saw a **dream in which he was sacrificing his son Ismail (AS)**
- He immediately understood its significance — **the dreams of Prophets are a form of revelation (Wahi) from Allah**
- He made up his mind to act upon it without hesitation
- This was one of the **greatest tests ever given to any human being**

---

## 👨‍👩‍👦 Preparing for the Sacrifice

- The next morning, Ibrahim (AS) asked his wife **Hajra (AS)** to bathe Ismail and dress him well
- Hajra (AS) lovingly bathed her son, applied fragrance, and got him ready
- Ibrahim (AS) then **took his son toward a mountain**, carrying the weight of the most painful decision of his life

---

## 😈 Shaytan's Three Failed Attempts

This is one of the most powerful parts of the story. Shaytan tried three times to stop the sacrifice:

### Attempt 1 — Targeting Hajra (AS)
- Shaytan appeared in the form of an **old man** before Hajra (AS)
- He told her: *"Ibrahim has not taken your son to a feast — he is going to sacrifice him!"*
- **Hajra's response:** *"If this is Allah's command, then we are fortunate. Even if I had a thousand sons like Ismail, I would happily sacrifice them all in Allah's path."*
- ✅ **Shaytan failed**

### Attempt 2 — Targeting Ismail (AS)
- Shaytan then went to **13-year-old Ismail (AS)** and said: *"Your father is not taking you to a feast — he is taking you to kill you!"*
- **Ismail's response:** *"If this is Allah's command, then what greater good fortune could there be for me than to be sacrificed in His path?"*
- ✅ **Shaytan failed again**

### Attempt 3 — Targeting Ibrahim (AS)
- Shaytan tried to weaken Ibrahim's resolve by saying:
  - *"You got this son after so many prayers — who will support you in old age?"*
  - *"What if Allah does not even accept this sacrifice? You will lose everything."*
- **Ibrahim's response:** He became angry and **threw stones at Shaytan**
- ✅ **Shaytan failed completely**

> 💡 **This is why during Hajj, pilgrims throw pebbles (Rami al-Jamarat) at the three pillars** — it commemorates Ibrahim (AS) throwing stones at Shaytan. It is one of the most important rituals of Hajj.

---

## 🤲 The Moment of Sacrifice

After Shaytan left, Ibrahim (AS) told Ismail (AS) everything. Ismail's response was extraordinary:

- **Ismail (AS) said:** *"Father, if this is Allah's command, I accept it fully. Just tie my hands and feet, and blindfold yourself — so that you do not hesitate when the moment comes."*
- He further said: *"When you lay the knife, please turn my face to the ground, so you cannot see my face and your hand does not stop."*

### What Happened Next:
- Ibrahim (AS) blindfolded himself, tied Ismail's hands and feet
- He placed the knife on Ismail's neck and pushed with full force
- **The knife did not cut** — by Allah's will, it was prevented
- Ibrahim (AS) heard the sound of a ram (Dumba)
- He removed his blindfold and saw — **a ram had been slaughtered in place of Ismail**, and Ismail stood at a distance, smiling

---

## ✨ Allah's Wisdom & Mercy

- Allah **never intended to take Ismail's life** — it was a test of faith and devotion
- Allah sent **Jibrail (AS)** with a beautiful ram from Jannah as a substitute
- This showed that **Allah is not a cruel king** — He tests His servants but does not harm them
- Ibrahim (AS) passed the greatest test with complete submission

---

## 🕌 The Tradition of Qurbani (Sacrifice)

- Allah was so pleased with Ibrahim's devotion that He made **Qurbani obligatory for all future generations**
- Every year, Muslims around the world **sacrifice animals on Eid ul-Adha** to keep alive the spirit of Ibrahim's (AS) Sunnah
- The purpose is to **develop the willingness to sacrifice what is dearest to us** for the sake of Allah

---

## 👶 The Reward — Birth of Ishaq (AS)

- As a reward for this immense sacrifice, **Allah gave Ibrahim (AS) the glad tidings of another son — Ishaq (AS)**
- At this time, Ibrahim (AS) was **120 years old** and Sara (AS) was **90 years old**
- Sara (AS) was astonished: *"How can I give birth when I am barren and my husband is old?"*
- The angels replied: **"Do not be surprised at Allah's decree — this is His mercy upon you"**

---

## 🕋 Construction of the Kaaba

- Ibrahim (AS) used to visit Ismail (AS) in Makkah regularly
- On one visit, Allah commanded Ibrahim (AS): **"Build My House at this place"**
- Ismail (AS) immediately agreed: *"I am ready"*
- **Together, father and son built the Kaaba**, and while raising its foundations, they prayed:

> *"O our Lord, accept this effort from us. You are the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing. Make us obedient to You, and from our descendants raise a nation that submits to You. Teach us the ways of worship and forgive us."*

### Special Miracles During Construction:
| Miracle | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Hajr-e-Aswad** | A stone sent from Jannah — it was originally pure white, but turned black due to the sins of mankind |
| **Maqam-e-Ibrahim** | Ibrahim (AS) stood on a stone while building — the stone miraculously softened and preserved his footprints; visible to this day |

---

## 📢 The Call to Hajj

- After the Kaaba was built, Allah commanded Ibrahim (AS): **"Announce Hajj to all people"**
- Ibrahim (AS) asked: *"O Allah, how far will my voice reach?"*
- Allah replied: **"Your duty is only to call out — delivering it is Our responsibility"**
- Ibrahim (AS) climbed Mount Arafat and called out:
  > *"O People! Come and perform the Hajj of Baitullah!"*
- Allah placed the **love of this House in the hearts of people for all eternity**
- That is why, to this day, millions of people desire and perform Hajj and Umrah every year

---

## 🌹 Final Years & Legacy

- Ibrahim (AS) passed away at the age of **175 years**
- He faced countless trials throughout his life — yet remained **completely faithful to Allah in every one of them**
- Because of his extraordinary devotion, Allah honored him with the title:

> ### 🏅 **"Khalilur Rahman"** — The Friend of Allah

---

## 📌 Key Lessons from This Story

| # | Lesson |
|---|---|
| 1 | **True faith means submitting to Allah even in the hardest moments** |
| 2 | **Shaytan always tries to weaken our resolve — we must recognize and reject his whispers** |
| 3 | **When we give up something for Allah, He replaces it with something even better** |
| 4 | **The purpose of Qurbani is not just slaughtering an animal — it is developing the spirit of sacrifice** |
| 5 | **Allah tests those He loves the most** |
| 6 | **A mother's faith (Hajra) and a child's courage (Ismail) are just as inspiring as the father's devotion** |
