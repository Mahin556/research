## What is Jumu‘ah?
- **Jumu‘ah (الجمعة)** is the special **Friday congregational prayer**.
- It replaces **Dhuhr (Zohar)** on Friday for eligible Muslim men.
- It is prayed in congregation with a **Khutbah (sermon)** before the prayer.
---
## Time of Jumu‘ah
- Same time as **Dhuhr**:
    - Starts after the sun passes its highest point (Zawal).
    - Ends when Asr time begins.
---
## Who Must Pray Jumu‘ah?
Obligatory for:
- Adult Muslim men
- Residents (not travelers)
- Physically able
Not obligatory for:
- Women (can pray Dhuhr instead)
- Children
- Travelers
- Sick people
---
## Rak‘ahs of Jumu‘ah

|Type|Rak‘ahs|Status|
|---|---|---|
|Sunnah (Before)|4|Sunnah|
|Fard (Jumu‘ah)|2|Obligatory|
|Sunnah (After)|4|Sunnah Mu’akkadah|
|Nafl (Optional)|2|Optional|

### Total = 12 Rak‘ahs (Minimum required = 2 Fard)
---
## 🗣️ Khutbah (Sermon)
Before the 2 Fard rak‘ahs:
- Imam gives **two Khutbahs**.
- Must listen silently.
- Talking during khutbah reduces reward.
---
## How to Pray Jumu‘ah
1️⃣ Pray 4 Sunnah before (optional but recommended).  
2️⃣ Listen to Khutbah silently.  
3️⃣ Pray 2 Fard behind Imam (recited aloud).  
4️⃣ Pray 4 Sunnah after.  
5️⃣ Optional 2 Nafl.

---
## Recitation
- 2 Fard rak‘ahs are recited **aloud** by the Imam.
- Sunnah prayers are silent.
---
## Virtues of Jumu‘ah
- Best day of the week.
- Sins forgiven between two Fridays.
- Special hour on Friday when duas are accepted.
- Surah Al-Kahf recommended to recite.
