### Janaze ki Namaz kya hai?
- Janaze ki namaz **dua wali namaz** hoti hai.
- Isme **ruku aur sajda nahi hota**.
- Sab log khade hokar padhte hain.
- Ye **farz-e-kifayah** hai (agar kuch log padh lein to sab par se zimmedari utar jaati hai).
---
### Kitni Takbeer Hoti Hai?
Janaze ki namaz me **4 Takbeer** hoti hain.

---
### Kaise Padhi Jaati Hai? (Step by Step)

#### Niyyat

"नीयत की मैंने 4 तकबीर नमाज़-ए-जनाज़ा, फर्ज़ किफाया, इस मय्यत (मृतक) के लिए, पीछे इस इमाम के"

---
#### Pehli Takbeer
- Imam "Allahu Akbar" kahega.
- Haath baandh lo.
- **Sana (Subhanaka Allahumma...)** padho.
---
####  Dusri Takbeer
- Phir Imam Allahu Akbar kahega.
- **Durood-e-Ibrahim** padho.
---
####  Teesri Takbeer
- Phir Allahu Akbar.
- **Janaze ki Dua** padho.
Example short dua:
"Allahummaghfir lahu warhamhu wa aafihi wa'fu anhu..."
(Agar aurat ho to lahu ki jagah laha.)

---
####  Chauthi Takbeer
- Phir Allahu Akbar.
- Thodi der ruk kar **Salam pher do** (right side, phir left side).
---
### Important Points
- Ruku aur Sajda nahi hota.
- Sirf khade hokar padhna hota hai.
- Agar mayyat bachcha ho to alag dua hoti hai.
- Qabrastan me bhi padh sakte hain.
