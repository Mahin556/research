### English Translation

1. Say, “O disbelievers,
    
2. I do not worship what you worship.
    
3. Nor are you worshippers of what I worship.
    
4. Nor will I be a worshipper of what you have worshipped.
    
5. Nor will you be worshippers of what I worship.
    
6. For you is your religion, and for me is my religion.”

---
### Hinglish Translation

1. Keh do: Ay kafiro!
    
2. Main un ki ibadat nahin karta jin ki tum ibadat karte ho.
    
3. Aur na tum us ki ibadat karne walay ho jis ki main ibadat karta hoon.
    
4. Aur na main us ki ibadat karne wala hoon jis ki tum ne ibadat ki.
    
5. Aur na tum us ki ibadat karne walay ho jis ki main ibadat karta hoon.
    
6. Tumhare liye tumhara deen hai aur mere liye mera deen hai.
