## What is Asr?
- **Asr** is the **third daily prayer** in Islam.
- It is offered in the **late afternoon**.
- It is a very important and emphasized prayer.
---
## Time of Asr
There is a difference of opinion between schools:
- **Shafi‘i, Maliki, Hanbali:**  
    Starts when shadow of an object equals its length. 
- **Hanafi:**  
    Starts when shadow becomes **twice** the object’s length.

Ends: **Just before sunset**.
⚠️ It is disliked (makruh) to delay Asr until the sun becomes very yellow.

---
## Rak‘ahs in Asr

| Type                      | Rak‘ahs | Status     |
| ------------------------- | ------- | ---------- |
| Sunnah (Ghair Mu’akkadah) | 4       | Optional   |
| Fard                      | 4       | Obligatory |
### Total = 8 Rak‘ahs
Minimum required: **4 Fard**

---
## Importance of Asr
- Allah mentions it specially in the Qur’an:  
    “Guard strictly the prayers, especially the middle prayer.”  
    (Many scholars say the “middle prayer” is Asr.)
- Missing Asr intentionally is considered a serious sin.
- Angels change shifts at Fajr and Asr.

---
## Recitation
- Asr is prayed **silently**.
- Imam recites silently in congregation.
---
## After Asr
- No Nafl prayer allowed after Asr until Maghrib.
- Only missed (Qaza) prayers are allowed.
