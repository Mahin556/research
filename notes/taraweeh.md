---

• **Taraweeh** Ramzan ke mahine me Isha ke baad padhi jati hai.  
• Ye **Sunnat-e-Muakkadah** hai (bohot important Sunnah).  
• Jamaat ke saath masjid me padhna zyada sawab wala hai, lekin ghar me bhi padh sakte hain.  
• Aksar 20 rakat padhi jati hai (Hanafi), kuch log 8 bhi padhte hain.

---
### Taraweeh Ka Tarika
• 2–2 rakat karke padhi jati hai.  
• Har 4 rakat ke baad thoda rest karte hain (isi liye naam “Taraweeh” = rest).  
• Normal namaz ki tarah ruku, sajdah sab hota hai.  
• Quran ka poora khatam (Khatm-e-Quran) bhi Taraweeh me hota hai.
