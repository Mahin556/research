Monotheism is the belief in one supreme God who is the creator and ruler of the universe, often seen as all-powerful, all-knowing, and morally perfect.  Major religions based on monotheism include Judaism, Christianity, and Islam, which emphasize exclusive worship of this single deity and often rely on sacred scriptures like the Bible or Quran for moral and spiritual guidance.
