• Choose **one habit only**. Not five. Not three. One. (Example: 20 minutes coding daily, 30 minutes workout, 20 minutes meditation.)

• Convert it into an **identity statement**:
“I am a person who ___ every day at ___ for 90 days.”

• Make it **public or socially accountable**. Tell friends, post it, or commit to someone you respect.

• Fix a **non-negotiable time**. Same time. Every single day. No switching.

• Fix the **exact method**. Same duration. Same structure. Same order. No variation.

• Remove decision points. Prepare everything the night before (clothes, book, mat, laptop, etc.).

• Create a **minimum version rule** (for bad days).
Example: If sick → 2 push-ups.
If tired → 5 minutes only.
The rule: Never zero.

• Pre-write obstacle solutions.
“If X happens, I will do Y.”
No thinking in the moment.

• Track streak, not intensity. Focus on **continuity**, not perfection.

• Commit to a fixed period (like 66–90 days). During this time, no quitting, no redesigning, no optimizing.
